How Y'all are Being Represented

Github Link Coming Soon

TL;DR

This project explored the usage of the Southern Dialect of English on politicians social media accounts, specifically the usage of the word y'all. Using a large sample of tweets from Georgia's representatives in the 115th Congress I found that even though the majority of Georgians use the word y'all, this word was only used 27 times across over 30,000 tweets indicating that the southern dialect may be avoided in the social media of politicians.
