About Me

I'm Aleksander, a Norwegian-American studying Quantitative Sciences, Math, and Computer Science at Emory University in Atlanta.

I have tons of interests. Everything from film to design to math and linguistics to music and maps. To me there is no end to what I want to explore.

To quote my favorite 70s Norwegian TV show:

Universet. En uendleighet av stjerner og planeter. Ingen vet hvor stort eller hvor gammelt det er.

- Brødrene Dal

To paraphrase, the universe is gigantic.

So there is plenty to explore.
