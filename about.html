about me

I'm Aleksander, a Norwegian-American MSc student in Applicable Mathematics at the London School of Economics and Political Science.

Previously I studied Quantitative Sciences, Math, and Linguistics at Emory University in Atlanta. While at Emory, I worked as a GIS Intern and Surveyor for the American Excavations of Samothrace and as a Digital Scholarship Intern for the Emory Center for Digital Scholarship. I also was a Teacher's Assistant for Statistical Computing I/II for Emory's Institute for Quantitative Theory and Methods.

I have tons of interests. Everything from film to design to math and linguistics to music and maps. I am currently learning more about music and visualizations along with my studies at the LSE.
